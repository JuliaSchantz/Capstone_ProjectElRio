OUR MISSION

Help foster youth feel seen and known through mentorship by the church.

Join us as we seek to care for post-foster care youth through mentorship.

By partnering with the church and local organizations, we want to help holistically grow, care for and empower those youth with the ultimate goal of helping them see the God who sees.

THE NEED

  • Children in Ohio foster care

  • Transition age youth in Ohio Foster care (16-21 year olds)

  • Of youth aging out of the system will experience homelessness

  • Up to 80% will have significant mental health issues

    • HOW CAN YOU HELP?

  • Mentor

    Walk alongside a youth transitioning out of the foster care system, building them up and investing in them as they grow and navigate this transition.

  • Pray

    Pray for the Lord’s guidance and protection for youth leaving the system. Pray for them to know the Lord and His love for them.

  • Donate

    Help support our mission to help foster youth feel seen and known.
