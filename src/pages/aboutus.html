What We're About

We are a Christian organization that is passionate about loving well and encouraging potential in invididuals who are transitioning out of foster care. We want to walk alongside them though this time of uncertainty and change, supporting them so they can intigrate into society and flourish as young adults.

WE BELIEVE

We believe no one is forgotten. It’s our mission, through mentorship, to show aging-out youth the love of Christ. Our hope is they will know they are seen and known by God and can find a purpose for their life by chosing to trust Him.

We believe these youth have incredible potential to impact society and encourage those younger than them that it’s possible to go against the statistics.

OUR NAME

El Roi’ is a name for God used in the Bible. It can be translated to “The God who sees me.” We chose this name because we felt it best represented our mission, to truly see and love youth in and transitioning out of the foster care system.

OUR LOGOMARK

We wanted our mark to be a symbol of growing together and incoorperate the eye for “God sees.” The two sprouts are meant to represent the youth and his or her mentor growing in the same direction, upward.
