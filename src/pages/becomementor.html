Becoming a Mentor

Steps to Becoming a Mentor

  • Pray

    Ask for the Lord’s guidance as you consider beginning the process of becoming a mentor. Pray that He would grow your desire for those in need, especially youth aging out of foster care.

  • Apply

    Tell us about yourself: your testimony, your story. Let us get to know you so we can see if you are a good fit for this ministry.

  • Interview

    Get to know us as we get to know more about you.

  • Train

    We want to equip you as you will soon begin mentoring. We want to guide you and give you the tools to mentor in the most beneficial and biblical way.

  • Meet

    Meet several youth aging out of the system to see if there is a good mentorship match. At this stage, you will be matched with at least one youth to mentor.

  • Mentor

    Begin mentoring and building a friendship, helping your mentee grow and learn how to navigate life in a healthy way while learning about the Lord!
